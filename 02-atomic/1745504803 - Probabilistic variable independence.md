# Probabilistic variable independence
> [!NOTE] Intro: 
> We say that two [[1745500044 - Random variables|Random variables]] A and B are independent if their union does not concide, then:
> $$
> P(A,B) = P(A)P(B) \Rightarrow P(A)P(B) = P(A) + P(B)
> $$
