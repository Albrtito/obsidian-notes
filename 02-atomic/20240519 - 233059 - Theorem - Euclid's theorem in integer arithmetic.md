> [!NOTE] Theorem: Euclid’s theorem 
> Given integers a,b ≠ 0, q and r. Such that following  [[20240519 - 214201 - Algorithm - The division algorithm|The division algorithm]]: 
> $$
> a = q\cdot b +r 
> $$ 
> with:  0 ≤ r < |b|
> **THEN:**
> + gcd(a,b) = gcd(b,r)
