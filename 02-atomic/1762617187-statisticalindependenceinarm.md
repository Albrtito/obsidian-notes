# statistical independence in ARM
> [!info] Intro: 
> We can use lift to look at the correlation between the items used in a rule. This way the confidence misleading rules can be discarted.

We look for items that have a positive correlation between them. Those where the lift is > 1. If the items of a rule have a negative lift then that point to a possible misleading confidence.
