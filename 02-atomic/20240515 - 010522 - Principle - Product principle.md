> [!NOTE] Product principle
> **Conditions:**
> + A and B are finite
> 
> **Result:**
> $|A * B| = |A| * |B|$
+ With multiple sets, this maintains. In this case $A_i$ are different sets.
 
$$
\prod^m_{k=1} |A_K|
$$
