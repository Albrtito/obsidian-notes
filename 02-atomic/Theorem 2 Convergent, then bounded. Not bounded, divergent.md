> [!NOTE] Theorem: 
> **Condition**: 
> + Sequence is convergent
> 
> **Consequence**: 
> + Sequence is bounded
>   
>$$
>   conv \rightarrow bound
>$$
> Logically we can negate both and swap positions: 
> 
>$$
> \lnot bound \rightarrow \lnot conv
>$$
> **Condition:**
> + Not bounded
> 
> **Consequence:**
> + Divergent

+ This theorem does not state any other relation than the two already mentioned
